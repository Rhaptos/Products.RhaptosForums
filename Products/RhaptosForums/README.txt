ForumLensmaker
Provides a content type that sub-classes the ContentSelectionLens in Lensmaker to provide the
ability to contain PloneboardForum types in the lens to allow discussion of the lens. This
product was created to keep dependencies on the Ploneboard product out of existing lenses.
